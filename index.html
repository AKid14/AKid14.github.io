About Lumbini

Lumbini is the birthplace of Lord Buddha, located in the Rupandehi District of Nepal. It is a UNESCO World Heritage Site known for its rich archaeological and spiritual significance. Key attractions include the Maya Devi Temple, Ashoka Pillar, World Peace Pagoda, and numerous monasteries built by various nations, symbolizing peace and harmony.
